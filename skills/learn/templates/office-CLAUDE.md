## Units & measurements

- Default to US customary (imperial) units ← or metric — pick one
- Architectural notation: feet-inches with dash (`12'-6"`), never decimal feet
- Always specify area type: GSF / USF / RSF / NSF — never a bare number
- Never mix unit systems within one table, calculation, or paragraph

## Terminology & abbreviations

- Use standard AEC terms (program, adjacency, circulation, egress, millwork)
- Define every abbreviation on first use, then abbreviate: "Gross Square Feet (GSF)" → "GSF"

## Codes & sources

- Cite codes with edition year and section: `IBC 2021 §1004.5`, never "the building code"
- Note that codes are adopted and amended locally: "(check local amendments)"
- Say "appears consistent with [section]" — never "complies with code"
- Every data point from an external source gets a citation

## Documents

- Descriptive filenames: `maple-st-zoning-summary.md`, never `output.md` or `doc1.md`
- Tables for all comparative data; units in column headers, not in every cell
- Site-visit report format: {header, observations, open items — in your order; "observed", never "inspected"} ← edit
